## 2025-05-15 - [Title character counter and header accessibility]
**Learning:** Marketplace titles often have strict character limits (e.g. 80 chars for eBay). Providing real-time visual feedback with color-coded thresholds (amber at 70, red at 80) helps users optimize listings without trial-and-error during the publishing phase.
**Action:** Always include character counters for fields with external platform constraints. Add accessibility labels and roles to all header navigation and action elements to ensure full screen-reader support.
